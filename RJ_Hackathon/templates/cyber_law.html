Rajasthan Police Hackathon

        
Identity theft

        as per section 66 C of the constitution Whoever, 
        fraudulently or dishonestly make use of the electronic signature, password or any other unique identification feature of any other person.
        Punishment:
        Upto 3 years of prison
        Fine upto 1 lakh.

        
Violation of Privacy

        As per IT act section 66E whoever intentionally or knowingly captures ,publishes or transmits image of a person without his or her consent .
        They shall be punished with :
        Imprisonment upto 3 years 
        Upto 2 lakhs fine

        
Infringement copy of copyrighted work:

        As per IT act of section 65 if any person knowing makes or has in his possession a copy of copyrighted property ,without the owners consent .
        They are punishable by:
        3 years in prison
        Fine upto 2 lakhs .

        
Personation Crimes:

        As per IT ACT SECTION  66D if a person is said to personate another person by any means of communication device or computer resource ,
        They are punished by :
        Imprisonment upto 3 years.
        fine of 1 lakhs .


        
Damage of Individual Computer ,Computer System or a network:

        As per IT ACT SECTION 43 If a person accesses ,downloads ,extracts data or contaminates viruses or other foreign means into a computer,computer system or network…

        They are punishable by :
        Imprisonment of upto  3 years 
        Fine upto 5,00,000 or both ..
